About

  • 2010-2014

    Undergraduate Education

    I graduated from University of California, Irvine with a degree in Public Health Sciences. While at Irvine I worked at UC Irvine Human Resources as a Worklife and Wellness Intern. During that time, we were moving our website to a new, more improved site. Although I wasn't working on the actual coding, my fellow intern showed me what he had been working on and I began my interest in Web Development and eventually made the transition.

  • 2014

    Human Resources

    After graduation, I worked in the health field at a Biotech company in their Human Resources Department. A combination of trying to keep within the field that I studied and my work experience. During my spare time I began learning basic html/css and Javascript and realized it was more interesting than the work I was currently doing for my company.

  • 2015

    Transition to Developer

    Decided to go with what I wanted to do in life and commited to becoming a developer and enrolled at Coding Dojo. Decided on Coding Dojo because they offered 3 stacks, that way I could a more immersive education. I was able to learn LAMP stack, Mean Stack, and iOS Developemnt with swift and xcode.

  • 2016

    Currently

    I am sharpening my skills as well as continuing to learn more on my own and diving deeper into each stack on my own. Aspiring to be a developer!
